Neptune

The farthest planet - an ice giant with powerful winds.

Image: Neptune

Overview

Neptune is an ice giant with a deep blue color. It has some of the fastest winds in the Solar System and can form large storm systems.

Main facts

Planet type
Ice giant
Winds
Extremely fast
Color
Blue (methane + atmosphere)
Moons
Many (including Triton)

Fun facts

  • Triton (its largest moon) orbits in the opposite direction of most moons.
  • Neptune takes about 165 Earth years to orbit the Sun.
  • It was discovered using mathematics before it was seen in a telescope.
