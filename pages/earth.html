Earth

Our home planet - liquid water, an atmosphere, and life.

Image: Earth

Overview

Earth is the third planet from the Sun. It has oceans of liquid water, a protective atmosphere, and a magnetic field that helps shield the surface from solar radiation.

Fun facts

  • About 70% of Earth's surface is covered by water.
  • The Moon stabilizes Earth's tilt, which influences seasons.
  • Earth's atmosphere helps keep temperatures suitable for life.
