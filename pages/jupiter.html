Jupiter

The largest planet - a gas giant with powerful storms.

Image: Jupiter

Overview

Jupiter is a gas giant made mostly of hydrogen and helium. It has many moons and a famous storm called the Great Red Spot.

Fun facts

  • Jupiter's magnetic field is extremely strong.
  • It spins very fast - a day is about 10 hours.
  • Some of its moons (like Europa) may have oceans under ice.
