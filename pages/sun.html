The Sun

A medium-sized star that powers the entire Solar System.

Image: The Sun

Overview

The Sun is a star made mostly of hydrogen and helium. Its gravity keeps planets in orbit, and its energy (light and heat) comes from nuclear fusion in its core.

Main facts

Type
G-type main-sequence star (G dwarf)
Energy source
Nuclear fusion (hydrogen -> helium)
Role
Provides gravity + light + heat
Surface activity
Sunspots, flares, solar wind

Fun facts

  • Light from the Sun takes about 8 minutes to reach Earth.
  • The Sun's outer atmosphere is called the corona.
  • Solar wind can affect satellites and create auroras on Earth.
