Saturn

A gas giant famous for its spectacular rings.

Image: Saturn

Overview

Saturn is a gas giant made mostly of hydrogen and helium. Its rings are made of ice and rock particles, ranging from tiny grains to large chunks.

Main facts

Planet type
Gas giant
Rings
Very bright and wide
Moons
Many (including Titan)
Density
Less dense than water

Fun facts

  • Titan, Saturn's largest moon, has a thick atmosphere.
  • Saturn's rings are extremely thin compared to how wide they are.
  • Like Jupiter, Saturn spins fast and has a short day.
