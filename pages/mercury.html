Mercury

The smallest planet and the closest to the Sun.

Image: Mercury

Overview

Mercury is a rocky planet with a surface full of craters, similar to Earth's Moon. It has almost no atmosphere, so it cannot hold heat well and temperatures change a lot between day and night.

Fun facts

  • Mercury has the shortest year of any planet because it orbits the Sun fastest.
  • Even though it's closest to the Sun, it's not the hottest planet (Venus is hotter).
  • Some craters near its poles may contain water ice.
